Teacher life in the classroom

Teachers in the SEP Program are as new to the program as any teacher curious in participating which means that any new teacher coming into the program won't be very behind and having to study late nights to catch up. Sometimes during our presentation or code alongs we even surprise our teacher with a line of code that they did not know and it's clear that they don't have a mastery over it yet, giving new teachers more confidence that they are not behind but eventually you will have to go out of your "comfort zone" and into the "panic zone" or "learning zone" to teach new material to the students in your class.

Code alongs

Code alongs mean that the teacher will create incomplete works of code and code based on their judgement but will also take in opinions from the students watching along. It is a great way to teach coding to new students and a great way to learn from the students as well since they might know of a shortcut or more efficient way of fixing the code.
